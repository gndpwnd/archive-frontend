Preserving A Valuable Resource

The internet today is filled with endless hours worth of seemingly inspirational and motivational content. However this poses a problem as a viewer can become overwhelmed with the decision of what and where to learn. On top of that viewers must figure out how and when to apply what they learn.

The goal of this archive is to focus on collecting the before-mentioned content on the internet and sort through the best parts. This archive will ultimately provide a centralized repository dedicated to the critical knowledge of today’s world.
